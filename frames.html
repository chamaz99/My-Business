Custom Frame Details

How Custom Pricing Works

Custom frames are priced based on the following factors:

  • Base Price: $120.00 for the first 100 square inches
  • Additional Area: $1.50 per additional square inch
  • Material Upgrade: Metal frames add $20.00
  • UV Glass: Adds $15.00 to protect from sunlight
Turnaround Time

Custom frames typically take 7-14 business days to produce, depending on complexity and current order volume.

What's Included
  • Handcrafted frame in your chosen material and color
  • High-quality glass (standard or UV-protective)
  • Acid-free mat board (optional, additional cost)
  • Acid-free backing to protect your photo
  • Ready-to-hang hardware installed
  • Protective packaging for shipping
